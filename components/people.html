Faculty

Dr. Zitong Yu

Principal Investigator

Associate Professor (Tenured) at Great Bay University, leading the YU Vision (YUV) Group. Previously a Postdoctoral researcher at ROSE Lab, Nanyang Technological University, and a visiting scholar at TVG, University of Oxford.

PhD from University of Oulu (2022). Recipient of Excellent Young Scholars (Overseas) Fund (国家优青海外). Research interests include computer vision, biometric security, and multimodal learning.

Serves as Associate Editor for Machine Intelligence Research (MIR) and Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence, and CAE for IEEE TIFS. Published papers in top venues including TPAMI, IJCV, TIP, CVPR, ICCV, etc.
